How it works

  • + icon (chevron right) to the left of parent categories indicates expandable items
  • Click the parent to expand/collapse subcategories
  • Subcategories are indented under the parent with smaller text
  • Chevron rotates 90 degrees when expanded
  • Items without subcategories (Home, Notifications) have no expand icon — they link directly
  • The "Favorites" group is shown expanded by default as an example
